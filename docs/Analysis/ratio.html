〖Ratio〗

Overview​

  • This sheet displays key ratios such as PER, PBR, and ROE for the input stocks.
  • Use it to analyze financial ratios within a sector and spot potentially undervalued stocks.

Provided Ratios and Calculation Methods​

  • PER (Price to Earnings Ratio):

    PER=Stock PriceEarnings per Share (EPS)\text{PER} = \frac{\text{Stock Price}}{\text{Earnings per Share (EPS)}}PER=Earnings per Share (EPS)Stock Price​

  • PBR (Price to Book Ratio):

    PBR=Stock PriceBook Value per Share (BPS)\text{PBR} = \frac{\text{Stock Price}}{\text{Book Value per Share (BPS)}}PBR=Book Value per Share (BPS)Stock Price​

  • PSR (Price to Sales Ratio):

    PSR=Market CapitalizationRevenue\text{PSR} = \frac{\text{Market Capitalization}}{\text{Revenue}}PSR=RevenueMarket Capitalization​

  • P/FCF (Price to Free Cash Flow):

    P/FCF=Market CapitalizationFree Cash Flow\text{P/FCF} = \frac{\text{Market Capitalization}}{\text{Free Cash Flow}}P/FCF=Free Cash FlowMarket Capitalization​

  • ROE (%) (Return on Equity):

    ROE=Net IncomeShareholders’ Equity×100\text{ROE} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Shareholders' Equity}} \times 100ROE=Shareholders’ EquityNet Income​×100

  • ROA (%) (Return on Assets):

    ROA=Net IncomeTotal Assets×100\text{ROA} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Total Assets}} \times 100ROA=Total AssetsNet Income​×100

  • D/E (%) (Debt to Equity Ratio):

    D/E=Total LiabilitiesShareholders’ Equity×100\text{D/E} = \frac{\text{Total Liabilities}}{\text{Shareholders' Equity}} \times 100D/E=Shareholders’ EquityTotal Liabilities​×100

Notes​

  • The 〖Ratio〗 sheet does not support ETF analysis.
  • Ratios related to income (earnings/returns), revenue (sales), and free cash flow are calculated on a TTM (trailing twelve months) basis.
  • Ratios related to equity (book value), assets, and debt are calculated on an MRQ (most recent quarter) basis — except when compared with income, in which case they are calculated on a TTM basis.
  • In summary: PER, PSR, P/FCF, ROE, and ROA are based on TTM, while PBR and D/E are based on MRQ.

Cell Guide​

  • Gray: Enter the stock identifiers here.
  • Dropdowns ▾ (E5–H5): Select the ratios you want to analyze.
  • Yellow: Displays today's price. If today is not a trading day, the most recent available data is shown.
  • Green: Shows the selected ratios for the input stocks.

How to Use​

  • Copy stock identifiers from the Stock List Sheets and paste them into the Gray cells.
    • Copy from〖jp〗

    • Paste to〖CAGR〗

  • In cells E5 to H5, select the ratio you want to analyze.

  • Click the Run button in the Longsight Sidebar to start the analysis.
